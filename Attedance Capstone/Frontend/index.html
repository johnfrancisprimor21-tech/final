What It Can Do

Everything You Need

Built with ASP.NET Core backend and a clean vanilla JS frontend

Secure Authentication
Username and password login with session tokens. Forgot password recovery via security questions.
Attendance Tracking
Students check in with status Present, Absent, Late, or Excused with date and course details.
Reports & Stats
View weekly and monthly attendance reports. Track streaks, rates, and overall performance at a glance.
Course Management
Students can add and manage their enrolled courses, including instructor info and schedules.
Teacher Dashboard
Teachers can view and mark attendance for their students, manage sections and generate summaries.
Customizable UI
Switch between dark and light themes and choose your preferred accent color. Settings persist across sessions.
